The Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2025 Architect Professional certification exam is a professional-level practical exam consisting of two sections: practical exercises and multiple-choice questions. To pass the exam, you must achieve a passing score in all sections. The Oracle Cloud Infrastructure
2025 Certified Architect Professional certification demonstrates your practical experience and knowledge required to plan, design, implement, and operate solutions on OCI. The certification exam covers the following topics:

• Designing scalable and resilient solutions for high availability and disaster recovery
• Designing cloud-native, microservices, and serverless architectures
• Delivering infrastructure as code
• Designing, implementing, and operating Oracle Database Services
• Designing hybrid and multi-cloud architectures
• Migrating workloads, databases, and data to OCI
• Implementing observability solutions

Completion of the latest training courses and on-the-job experience are recommended.
